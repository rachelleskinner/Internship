Mental Health Wellness

Feeling anxious or stressed? Here are some simple mindfulness breathing exercises to consider:

Box Breathing

Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds, hold in for 4 seconds, exhale through your mouth for 4 seconds, hold out for 4 seconds.

Belly Breathing

Start one hand on your chest and one hand on your belly below the ribcage. Inhale through your nose while your stomach rises with a neutral chest. Exhale through your mouth while your belly moves back down to neutral.

Repeat these exercises until relaxed.
